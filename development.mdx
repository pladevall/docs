## Managing Your Subscription

Only administrators have access to billing management and plan cancellation features.

## Viewing Plan Details

To access your subscription information:
1. Navigate to **Settings**
2. Select the **Billing** tab

Here you'll find a comprehensive overview of your current Index plan and complete billing history[1].

## Subscription Management

To cancel your Index Business Plan:
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click on the **Billing** tab
3. Look for the cancellation option in your subscription details[1]

**Note**: All billing-related actions require administrator privileges[1].
